Moon

The Moon is Earth’s loyal, slightly aloof sidekick, who shows up for every night shift but never stays for the after-party. It’s about 1/4th the size of Earth, but it still manages to pull off some serious gravitational tricks, like causing tides and occasionally giving us a solar eclipse just to make sure we’re paying attention. The Moon’s surface is a dusty, crater-filled wasteland, a perfect reminder that if you don’t have an atmosphere, life as we know it becomes a bit... dusty. It also doesn’t have any weather or a breathable atmosphere, which is probably why it never gets invited to any cool, Earthly parties.
