Earth

The Earth is the slightly chaotic, middle child of the solar system. It's a waterlogged rock spinning at 1,000 miles per hour while hurtling around the Sun at 67,000 miles per hour—no wonder it has a lot of mood swings, from tornadoes to earthquakes. It’s got a breathable atmosphere, just the right amount of gravity to keep your coffee from floating away, and a diverse collection of ecosystems that somehow include both penguins and camels. In short, Earth’s the planet equivalent of an overachiever trying to balance a bunch of different hobbies... with occasional volcanic tantrums.
